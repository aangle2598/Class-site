First Scene

Morgan and Angel wake up, it's 2009. They both get out of bed and brush their teeth at the same time. They go outside after saying bye to their parents and ride bikes with their friends for a little.

Second Scene

2011. Angel comes inside from playing outside, and meets his dad. They draw together. At the same time morgan is playing videogames.

Third Scene

2014. Morgan flies on a plane with an urn with his family to Jamaica. Morgan releases the ashes of his father while Angel is home playing videogames.

Fourth Scene

2016. Morgan and Angel both move to College at different times. They hang out with their friends that they meet and wind up passing each other blindly while doing so.

Fifth Scene

2017. Morgan and Angel finally meet, they unpack and the line between the two separate. Scene changes to the both of them hanging out and playing games together, talking and telling stories. They climb trees and ride down a hill on a bike and longboard. Angel is in class and gets the news of his dad passing. Morgan comes to comfort him and give a hug. Pans to the funeral. Angel looks at wall with picture of his father on it.

Seventh Scene

2019. Morgan is watching scene of Naruto where Sasuke explains how they both grew up without families. Angel comforts morgan. Pans to them getting their shit together and climbing a tree, handshake.
